About the City

Appalachian City is a vibrant semi-coastal city nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains in New Brunswick. Known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and friendly community, it offers a unique blend of outdoor adventures and urban experiences.

Attractions

  • Mountain View Park
  • Coastal Boardwalk
  • Appalachian Art Museum
  • Annual Harvest Festival
