About Rivermount

Rivermount is a vibrant semi-coastal city nestled in the Appalachian Mountains of New Brunswick, blending mountain adventure with maritime charm.

History

Founded in the 1800s, Rivermount grew as a hub for trade and culture, shaped by its unique geography and diverse communities.
